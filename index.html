School History

Republic Act No. 9865 dated December 2, 2010 converted and named Pines City National High School - Magsaysay Annex into MAGSAYSAY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL. After 21 years of fruitful existence with Pines City National High School, Magsaysay Annex is now an independent national high school. MNHS was a joint venture of the late couple Dr. Philip M. Flores, then the Principal of PCNHS, and Nelda Flores, then Barangay Captain of New Lucban. They were supported by the New Lucban Sto.
